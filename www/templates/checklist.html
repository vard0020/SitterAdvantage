The following is a list of questions you should ask yourself when you are asked to be a sitter:

Do you know these people?

Do your parents know them?

Does someone you know, know them?

How many kids will you look after?

What else would they like you to do?

When do they need you?

Start time?

End time?

Address?

How will you get there?

How will you get back home?

How much did they offer to pay you?

Did you tell them your rate of pay?

Will you meet for a pre-job meeting?

If so, when?
