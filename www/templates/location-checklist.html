The following is a list of questions you should be aware of when you arrive.

DO NOT be afraid to ask the parents questions. If they respond with a “don’t worry about it” type of question, politely ask for an answer.

DO NOT rely on what the kids tell you. It’s a good idea to arrive early.

The following is the information you should input into SitterAdvantage

The parents’ names and contact information

The kid’s names, birthdate, allergies, handicaps or disabilities

Take a clear photo of each kid

Enter and schedule tasks and set appropriate alarms that may include:
     - Bedtime
     - When to give medication

It is important that all the information is entered in case of an emergency.

Although they may have answered your questions before, there may have been changes since you last spoke so it’s okay to ask the same questions again for clarification.

Questions you should ask the client

Do you get cell phone service in the house?

Is there WiFi in the house? If it is a week WiFi signal, do not use.

Where are the exits leading outside?

Where are the telephones?

Do you have a first aid kit and where is it located?

Do you have a fire extinguisher and where is it located?

Do you have a fire escape procedure, route or meeting place in the event of an emergency?

Do you have a security system and can you show me how to use it including any panic buttons?

What is the security system passcode and password?

Am I expected to make food for the kids?

Are there any pets to be looked after? Cat or dog to let out?

What rooms are the kids allowed or not allowed to be in?

Do they have a favorite video, game, toy or other activity available?

Is there anything else you feel I should know about the home and the kids?

Is there a chance that you may be home later than expected?

Are you okay with my rate?

Will you bring me home afterwards or will I need to arrange to be picked up?

There may be other questions you may ask that are not listed here but don’t be afraid to ask them.
Keep in mind your checklist of things you DO NOT feel comfortable doing.
